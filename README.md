Animations for Koreader:

Includes animations for:

* Change of page in books
* Change of page in library (tested in simple UI)
* Opening and closing of books
* Blocking and unlocking Kindle
* Switch between library and home screen in Simple UI

To activate with an open book go to "touches and gestures" and "change of page"

Created with Claude and Gemini based on my previous patch, which was improved by https://github.com/Euphoriyy.

Tested on a Kindle PW3

I hope it will be useful and sorry that the animations are in Spanish, but I speak native Spanish and it is more comfortable for me to have it like this.
